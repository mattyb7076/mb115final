The Evolution of the Soccer Ball
Matan Belillti

Vulcanized Rubber Balls

-> Beginning of the 18th Century
-> First ball that used a solid rubber bladder to keep stability
-> Heavy, bulky, and uncomfortable to kick

Eight Panel Ball

-> 1930s Produced with leather and could bounce easier
-> Heavy stitching made it inconvenient to inflate and keep it inflated

18 Panel Ball

-> 1950s Orange ball invented to help spectators see in all kinds of inclement weather
-> Extra panels allow a more comfortable feel when kicking
-> First ball with small neat stitching to keep it inflated longer

1963 Santiago Ball

-> Created by Adidas
-> First official ball endorsed around the world
-> Lightweight leather design to enhance flight and bounce
-> Better water absorbtion to improve play in bad weather

1969 Buckminster Ball

-> Hexagon, pentagon, and triangle panel design created by Richard Buckminster Fuller
-> Consisting of 20 hexagonal and 12 pentagonal surfaces
-> First design when inflated, creates a perfect sphere
-> Black spots intended for players and spectators to anticipate the swerve of the ball

2000 and beyond

- Most still use the same Buckminster's design
- Customized designs to fit different clubs and cups
- Have optimum flight, soft feel, water proof, and transfers all kicking force effectively
